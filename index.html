The Destination

Pondicherry —
A City That Lingers

Few places in India hold the quiet elegance of Pondicherry. French colonial boulevards in White Town lined with bougainvillea. The scent of jasmine and sea salt along Promenade Beach. Whitewashed walls that carry centuries of Puducherry heritage. Whether you're exploring the French Quarter, meditating at Auroville, or watching the sunrise from Serenity Beach — Pondicherry is not just a destination. It is a feeling you return to.

Our Story

The soul of Pondicherry, curated for you

Pynest was born from a simple belief — that a stay should feel like an invitation into a place, not a transaction. Every villa in our collection is chosen, not listed.

Why Pynest

Built by people who love this coastline

Pynest started with one villa in White Town and a conviction that Pondicherry deserved a hospitality brand as considered as the town itself — French colonial streets, quiet beaches, and a slower rhythm of life.

Today, we work with a handpicked portfolio of private pool villas, beachfront estates, and heritage homestays, each personally inspected and managed to a single standard: would we stay here ourselves?
